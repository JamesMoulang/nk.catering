Ordering

Wholesale orders are on a weekly standing order basis. Please place any orders or adjustments by Monday afternoon for your order to be delivered on Friday.

The buns are delivered in reusable boxes, with allergen information and date labels. I'm a registered food business with a 5 star hygeine rating.

Minimum order per flavour: 32 buns

Wholesale prices available upon request.

For samples please order through the buns page.

Dietary info

All buns - with the exception of certain seasonal buns - are suitable for vegetarians. The Cheese, Marmite & Mustard buns are suitable for vegans.

I can make anything vegan on request - please get in touch about any allergens or dietary requirements!

Buns store well in the freezer for up to a month and can be reheated in the microwave or the oven.
